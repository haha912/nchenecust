Personal Information

Dr. Ning Chen has been committed to research in the domain of music information processing, digital audio authentication and music information retrieval technology , since she got Electrical Engineering Ph.D. in Shanghai Jiaotong University in 2005. She has published over 30 refereed papers (among which 12 papers were indexed by SCI, 30 papers were indexed by EI, were cited more than 130 times) in domestic and foreign authoritative journals and conferences, such as Electronics Letters, Applied Acoustics, IET Information Security, ETRI Journal, EURASIP Journal on Audio Speech and Music Processing, IET Communications, Science China, Journal of Zhejiang University, ISMIR and ICME. She has applied two patents and one formal authorization. She has successively received two National Natural Science Foundations and one Chinese postdoctoral fund as principal, and she has also participated in research work of two National Natural Science Foundations as main member.
